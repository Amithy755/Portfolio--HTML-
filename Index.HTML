Descriptin About Myself:

"I'm a quiet and friendly soul with a dash of chaotic energy. My love for internet novels fuels my imagination, and I pour that creativity into private short stories that I write solely for myself. Socializing and being in the spotlight? Not my thing—I thrive in the background, away from prying eyes. I’ve got a fiery palate for spicy and sour flavors but can’t stand anything overly sweet or bitter. My life mottos? ‘Live fast, eat carbs, die happy,’ and ‘Life’s not a journey—it’s a scavenger hunt for sanity.’ They sum up my philosophy: embrace the wild, savor the carbs, and find balance in the chaos.".
